## What You Can Do

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Create Cinema Experiences">
    Build massive television walls by connecting multiple TV blocks together. The mod automatically detects adjacent TVs and forms them into a single large screen. Perfect for movie theaters, sports arenas, or Times Square-style displays.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Broadcast Live Views">
    Place viewfinder blocks anywhere in your world and link them to hollow cassettes. Insert the cassette into a TV to watch a live feed from the viewfinder's location. Great for security systems, remote monitoring, or sharing scenic views.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Play Cassette Content">
    Collect cassette tapes and play them on your televisions. Each cassette contains unique animated content with synchronized audio. Find them in loot chests or craft your own hollow cassettes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Integrate with Other Mods">
    Vista includes compatibility with popular mods like Supplementaries, ComputerCraft, JEI, and more. The API allows other mod developers to add custom cassette content or interact with the broadcast system.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Requirements

- **Minecraft Version**: 1.21.1 - 1.21.2
- **Mod Loader**: Fabric or NeoForge
- **Dependencies**: 
  - Moonlight Lib (required)
  - Fabric API (Fabric only)

<Warning>
Vista is incompatible with Iris shaders. While the mod will function, you may experience visual issues and degraded performance even with shaders disabled. This is a known limitation due to Iris's architecture.
</Warning>

## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the mod">
    Download Vista from [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/amendments) or [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/vista_tv) and install with your mod loader.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Craft a television">
    Use the crafting recipe to create your first television block. TVs can be placed on any horizontal surface.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find or craft a cassette">
    Cassettes can be found in treasure chests or dropped by creepers killed by pillagers. You can also craft hollow cassettes to link to viewfinders.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Insert and watch">
    Right-click on a TV with a cassette to insert it. The content will start playing automatically. Right-click again to eject.
  </Step>
</Steps>
